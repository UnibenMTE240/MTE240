How to Book Hostel in UNIBEN

Hostel booking in UNIBEN is done online via the Kofa portal and is only available after your online clearance is approved.

Note: Hostel slots are limited and go fast! Booking is strictly first-come, first-served.

Requirements Before Booking

  • ✅ Clearance status must be “Cleared” on Kofa
  • ✅ Active student login on Kofa Portal
  • ✅ Recent passport uploaded
  • ✅ Funds available to pay hostel fee via Remita

Step 1: Login to Kofa Portal

Visit https://waeup.uniben.edu/login and log in with your student credentials.

Step 2: Go to “Hostel Accommodation”

From your dashboard, click on “Accommodation” or “Hostel Application” when the portal is open.

Step 3: Choose Hostel Type

Options vary based on gender, faculty, and price. Choose the one that fits your needs.

Step 4: Make Payment

Generate your Remita payment slip and pay online. Do not delay — slots are reserved only after successful payment.

Step 5: Print Your Hostel Slip

Once payment is confirmed, return to the portal and print your Hostel Allocation Slip.

You’re done! You’ll present your slip when moving into your hostel during resumption.

Estimated Hostel Prices

Hostel fees vary depending on the hall and the facilities available. Below are the typical estimates to help you prepare:

  • Halls 1–9 (Standard Hostels): ₦25,000 – ₦35,000
  • Clinical Hostels (Medical Campus): ₦40,000 – ₦45,000
  • Hall 10: ₦35,000 – ₦40,000
  • Postgraduate Hostels: ₦50,000 – ₦60,000
  • International Students Hostel: ₦70,000 – ₦80,000+

Note: These prices are based on past sessions and may be updated by the university. Always check your Kofa portal for the exact current fees.

Hostel Booking FAQs

  • When does hostel booking usually start?

    Hostel booking typically opens shortly after online clearance is completed. It will be announced on your Kofa dashboard — so check regularly!

  • Can I book a hostel before completing clearance?

    No. You must be fully cleared online before you’ll be allowed to access the accommodation section on Kofa.

  • Can I change my hostel after booking?

    Generally, hostel bookings are final and not changeable after payment. You can only request swaps in rare situations — contact your student affairs division.

  • What happens if I miss booking?

    If you miss booking, you may have to arrange off-campus accommodation. UNIBEN hostels are limited and filled on a first-come, first-served basis.

  • Is hostel payment done online?

    Yes. All payments are done via the Remita platform through the Kofa portal. After generating a Remita slip, you can pay online or at a bank.

  • Will I choose the hostel I want?

    Yes, but options are based on availability. You can only select from hostels with open slots for your gender and level.
