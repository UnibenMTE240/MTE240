How to Get Your Record File

UNIBEN requires every student to obtain a record file before completing physical clearance. Follow the official process below to get yours.

Note: The record file is not sold by vendors. It must be officially collected from the Exams and Records Unit after payment.

Step 1: Pay ₦2,000 on Remita

Go to www.remita.net and generate a payment for the record file under UNIBEN. Make sure you:

  • Select University of Benin as the institution
  • Choose “Record File” or “Student File” as the service
  • Pay the sum of ₦2,000
  • Print the Remita payment receipt after successful payment

Step 2: Convert to UNIBEN Official Receipt

Take your Remita payment receipt to the Bursary section inside UNIBEN to convert it into an official UNIBEN receipt. This is mandatory.

Step 3: Go to Exams & Records to Collect File

With your UNIBEN receipt, proceed to the Exams and Records Unit (usually inside the Faculty of Education building) and present it to collect your record file.

Step 4: Label and Arrange Your File

Write your full name, registration number, department, and faculty on the file. Neatly arrange your printed clearance documents inside.

✅ You’re now ready to proceed with physical clearance using your official record file.
